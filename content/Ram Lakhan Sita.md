

## Definition

The divine trio of:

- Ram: Lord Rama
- Lakhan: Lakshmana
- Sita: Goddess Sita

## Significance

- Central figures of Ramayana
- Objects of Hanuman's devotion
- Divine family

## Spiritual Meaning

- Perfect dharma
- Divine relationships
- Ideal governance
