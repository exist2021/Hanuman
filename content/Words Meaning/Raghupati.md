# Raghupati Bhakt

- Raghupati: Lord of Raghu dynasty (Rama)
- Bhakt: Devotee

## Role

- Perfect devotee
- Divine servant
- Spiritual exemplar

## Significance

- Model of devotion
- Perfect service
- Divine relationship
