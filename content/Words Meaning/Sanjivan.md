
# Sanjivan Buti
## Definition

Life-restoring herb

## Context in Ramayana

- Brought by Hanuman from Himalayas
- Used to revive Lakshmana
- Demonstration of devotion and power

## Significance

- Symbol of healing
- Divine intervention
- Hanuman's dedication
