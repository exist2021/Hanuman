# Man Kram Vachan

## Definition

- Man: Mind
- Kram: Action
- Vachan: Speech

## Spiritual Significance

- Complete devotion
- Integrated worship
- Total surrender

## Practice

- Alignment of thought, deed, and word
- Holistic devotion
- Spiritual integrity
