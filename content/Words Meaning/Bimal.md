# बिमल (Bimal)

## Definition
Pure, spotless, unblemished, immaculate

## Etymology
- Sanskrit: विमल (vimala)
- Prakrit: बिमल (bimala)
- Modern Hindi: बिमल/विमल (bimal/vimal)
