# Hari Chera

## Definition

- Hari: Lord (Rama)
- Chera: Servant/Devotee

## Spiritual Context

- Eternal servitude
- Divine relationship
- Perfect devotion

## Significance

- Humility in devotion
- Surrender to divine
- Service attitude
