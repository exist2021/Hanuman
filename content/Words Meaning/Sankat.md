# Sankat Mochan

## Definition

- Sankat: Trouble/Crisis
- Mochan: Reliever/Remover

## Role

- Reliever of devotees' troubles
- Protector in times of crisis
- Divine intercessor

## Significance in Worship

- Popular title for Hanuman
- Focus of many prayers
- Symbol of divine help
