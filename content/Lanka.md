# Lanka Dahan
## Definition

The burning of Lanka by Hanuman

## Historical Context

- Part of Ramayana epic
- Demonstration of Hanuman's power
- Message to Ravana

## Spiritual Significance

- Victory of good over evil
- Power of devotion
- Divine justice
